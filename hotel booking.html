Our History

Much like its history, the Palace presents a charming medley of colonial style, princely Indian culture and unexpected modern trendiness. A timeless courtesy and subtle sophistication reflect in every service lending credence to the visitor who said 'its too intimate to be called a place, too friendly to be called a hotel.'

Meetings & Events

The Jehan Numa team understands the importance of each individual event and provides personalized and professional service – just as they would for royal festivities. A pick of venues - both indoor and outdoor - outstanding food and beverage offerings, and expertly customized themes, décor, and music make each event special and memorable.
